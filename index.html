About Us

History

StoryUnit was founded in 2014 with a vision for a new kind of literary agency, one that would combine a passion for managing a writer's career with an integrated understanding of how storytelling works.

Achievements

During the 5 years of its existence, the agency has negotiated over 1,000 licenses for translation and publication or co-production of books for the local market. The number of licenses arranged by us per year exceeds 200.

Partners

The agency currently has partners with over 50 literary agencies and publishers from the United States, the United Kingdom, Germany and Russia, many of which are needed for Bulgaria, Macedonia and Albania.

Goals

Our goal is to maximize the value of our clients' work by providing hands-on editorial and marketing advice, as well as leading the way in branding and licensing.

Priorities & Strengths

Long-term relationships with our clients are a top priority in the work of the agency. Constant awareness, the ability to offer the right book to the right publisher, and the art of negotiation are our main strengths.
